    What can you expect from us?

    ICT Competence

    We have developed and applied ICT competencies that help us collaborate effectively, solve problems, and create innovative solutions. Each of us brings unique skills to the table, and together, we combine our strengths to tackle challenges in the digital world.

    Digital Literacy

    Our digital literacy skills have been progressed to enable effective navigation and use of technology. Digital literacy goes beyond simply using devices; it encompasses the ability to leverage digital tools to communicate, create, and collaborate.

    Communication Skills

    As professionals, improving strong communication skills is essential for success, both academically and in real-life scenarios. These skills are not just about speaking clearly, but about how to interact, collaborate, and ensure everyone is on the same page.

    Professionalism

    Professionalism is a skill that each member demonstrates through respect, responsibility, and commitment to their work. Professionalism is not just about how you behave in a job or internship setting, but also how you approach your schoolwork, etc.

      (Quarter 3)

      Stream outline

      Our stream outline shows the time and the allotment inside that time period, the topic, the activities, what's show inside the stream, and the audio. It is basically our plan for the actual stream and how we will execute it.


      Promotion Plan

      Our chosen way of promoting our stream is by making a poster and promoting it via our Facebook page online, and by sharing it to our friends and families. Poster consists of Jeeps and Taxi's since our stream is about transportation.

      Stream Design

      Our stream outline consists of our main colors such as yellow, and white. We added brown as contrast for the viewers to easily see where the borders are to have a clean looking layout.

      (Quarter 4)

      Stream outline #2

      This shows our 2nd stream plan for our 2nd stream. It includes time allotment for various activities that we plan to do inside the stream.


      Promotion Plan #2

      For our 2nd Promotion Plan, we considered promoting through Instagram Stories and multiple other platforms like Facebook via our Facebook page. Some materials were tailored to better fit those platforms.

      Stream Design #2

      This design uses warmer tones and images of buildings with a mix of pink, and yellow. symbolizing a sun set. as this stream is our 2nd and the last, we plan to make it look like the viewers are going home from somewhere.
